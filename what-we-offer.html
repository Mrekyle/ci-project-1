Why choose us?

When its finally time to decide on the perfect driving school for you. Here at First Class Pass we pride ourselves in having the most patient and intentive instructors possible. With a combined experiance of 20+ years of teaching. All our instructors are DBS checked and are fully qualified Approved Driving Instructors. (DSA ADI).

Offering lessons in Manual cars and Automatic cars

Weekly

We reccomend having a driving lesson at least once a week. Ideally with a 2 hour lesson. This will ensure that you are soaking up as much as possible and become as comfortable as you can before your test.

Towards the end of your lessons, your instructor will perform a mock test with you. This is to give you an idea of what to expect on the day and to see if you are ready to pass 1st time.

Intensive Lessons

With our intensive lessons, depending on the hours booked will define how quick the lessons are undertaken (Monday to Friday). During this style of lesson we reccomend to drive atleat 2 hours a day but no longer than 5. This is to ensure you get what you need to pass the test.
