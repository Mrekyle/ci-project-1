Colchester • Ipswich • Braintree • Clacton

First class Pass driving school was founded back in 2021. We have since become one of the most recognisable and well known schools in the Colchester area. We now offer lessons all around the Colchester, Ipswich, Braintree and Clacton area. Our team of dedicated instructors are able to teach either Manual or Automatic.


We are also able to help you with your theory test with access to Theory Test Pro, to assist in booking your theory and your practical tests when you are ready.
